Is this time really different? Impacts of COVID-19 on state and local economies and housing markets (with Jiyoung Chae, Ivan Caceres, and Geoffrey J.D. Hewings)

Summary

Focusing on the housing market in Illinois, the economic impacts of the period before and after the COVID-19 pandemic are compared to a comparable time during the Great Recession in 2008.
